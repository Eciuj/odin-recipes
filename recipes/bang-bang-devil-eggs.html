Bang Bang Deviled Eggs

A combination of kewpie mayonnaise, sweet Thai chili sauce, Sriracha, and rice vinegar takes classic deviled eggs to new heights.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs, hard-cooked
  • 2 tablespoons Japanese mayonnaise (such as Kewpie)
  • 2 tablespoons sweet Thai chili sauce (such as Mae Ploy)
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha chile sauce, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon paprika, for garnish

Directions

Step 1

  1. Peel hard-cooked eggs and then slice half lengthwise; remove yolks into a small mixing bowl.

  2. Step 2

    Gently mash yolks with a fork into a paste and then add your kewpie mayonnaise, sweet Thai chili sauce, and sriracha; mix well to combine.

  3. Step 3

    Spoon or pipe mixture into egg whites.

  4. Step 4

    Garnish eggs with paprika and chopped green onions. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
